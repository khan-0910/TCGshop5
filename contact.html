Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 day delivery. See our Shipping Policy for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Digital Wallets through Razorpay's secure payment gateway.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes! Orders can be cancelled before shipment. Contact us immediately at orders@pokecards-store.com with your order number.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can track it on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or defective items. See our Refund Policy for complete details.

Are the cards authentic?

Yes! All our Pokemon cards are 100% authentic. We source directly from authorized distributors and verify all products.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries. International shipping takes 10-21 business days. Customs fees may apply.

How do I check my order status?

Email us at orders@pokecards-store.com with your order number, and we'll provide an immediate update.
