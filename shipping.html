Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Shipping Methods & Costs

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost Tracking
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $4.99 Yes
Express Shipping 2-3 business days $9.99 Yes
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $19.99 Yes
Free Shipping 5-7 business days Free (orders over $50) Yes

2. Processing Time

  • Standard Orders: 1-2 business days
  • High-Value Orders (over $500): 2-3 business days (additional verification)
  • Pre-Orders: Ships on or after release date
  • Custom Orders: 3-5 business days

Note: Processing time is separate from shipping time. Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

3. Shipping Locations

3.1 Domestic Shipping (India)

  • We ship to all states and union territories
  • PO Boxes accepted
  • APO/FPO addresses accepted

3.2 International Shipping

  • Available to select countries
  • Delivery time: 10-21 business days
  • Customs fees and import duties are customer's responsibility
  • International orders may require additional documentation

4. Order Tracking

  • Tracking number provided via email once shipped
  • Track your order on our website or carrier's website
  • Updates available in real-time
  • Email notifications for key shipping milestones

5. Packaging

We take great care in packaging your Pokemon cards:

  • Individual Cards: Sleeved, top-loaded, and bubble-wrapped
  • Sealed Products: Bubble-wrapped and boxed
  • High-Value Items: Double-boxed with extra protection
  • Graded Cards: Special protective packaging
  • Eco-Friendly: Recyclable materials used when possible

6. Shipping Restrictions

We cannot ship to:

  • Countries under trade embargo
  • Addresses flagged for fraud
  • Locations where delivery is not possible

7. Delivery Issues

7.1 Lost Packages

  • Contact us if package is not delivered within expected timeframe
  • We will file a claim with the carrier
  • Replacement or refund provided once claim is approved

7.2 Damaged Packages

  • Inspect package upon delivery
  • Note any damage on delivery receipt
  • Contact us within 48 hours with photos
  • We will arrange replacement or refund

7.3 Incorrect Address

  • Verify shipping address before completing order
  • Contact us immediately if you notice an error
  • Address changes after shipment may incur additional fees
  • We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customer

8. Signature Requirements

  • Orders over $200: Signature required
  • Orders over $500: Adult signature required
  • High-Value Items: May require ID verification

9. Shipping Insurance

  • All orders automatically insured up to $100
  • Additional insurance available for high-value orders
  • Insurance covers loss and damage during transit
  • Claims must be filed within 30 days of shipment

10. Holidays and Delays

  • No shipping on major holidays
  • Delays possible during peak seasons (holidays, new releases)
  • Weather-related delays beyond our control
  • Carrier delays may occur without notice

11. Multiple Item Orders

  • Items may ship separately if from different warehouses
  • You will receive separate tracking numbers
  • No additional shipping charges for split shipments

12. Pre-Order Shipping

  • Pre-orders ship on or shortly after release date
  • Shipping method selected at time of order
  • Cannot combine pre-orders with in-stock items
  • Release dates subject to change by manufacturer

13. Expedited Shipping Cutoff

For guaranteed delivery times:

  • Overnight: Order by 12 PM EST
  • 2-Day: Order by 2 PM EST
  • Standard: Order by 5 PM EST

14. Undeliverable Packages

  • Packages returned as undeliverable will be refunded minus shipping costs
  • Customer responsible for providing correct address
  • Reshipment requires new shipping payment

15. Shipping Confirmation

You will receive email notifications for:

  • Order confirmation
  • Order processing
  • Shipment confirmation with tracking
  • Delivery confirmation

16. Contact for Shipping Questions

For shipping inquiries:

  • Email: shipping@pokecards-store.com
  • Support: support@pokecards-store.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
  • Include: Order number and tracking number

17. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this shipping policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
