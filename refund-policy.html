Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Order Cancellation

We understand that circumstances change. Here's our cancellation policy:

  • Before Shipment: Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they are shipped. Full refund will be processed.
  • After Shipment: Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. Please refer to our return policy.
  • How to Cancel: Contact us at support@pokecards-store.com with your order number.

2. Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our refund policy includes:

2.1 Eligible for Refund:

  • Damaged or defective cards upon arrival
  • Wrong item shipped
  • Item significantly not as described
  • Order cancelled before shipment

2.2 Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Change of mind after shipment
  • Cards opened from sealed packaging (unless defective)
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Items purchased on final sale or clearance

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Provide order number and reason for return
  3. Send clear photos of the item (if damaged/defective)
  4. Wait for return authorization
  5. Ship item back in original condition and packaging

4. Refund Timeline

  • Inspection: 2-3 business days after we receive the return
  • Approval: Email notification sent once approved
  • Processing: 5-7 business days to original payment method
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days depending on your bank

5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the same card
  • UPI: Refunded to the same UPI ID
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the same bank account
  • Wallets: Refunded to the same wallet

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in cases of:

  • Item returned without original packaging
  • Item showing signs of use
  • Item returned after 7 days but within 14 days

7. Shipping Costs

  • Our Error: We cover return shipping costs
  • Customer's Choice: Customer covers return shipping costs
  • Original Shipping: Non-refundable unless item is defective

8. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

9. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • No need to return the damaged item in most cases
  • Full refund including shipping costs

10. Contact Information

For cancellations, returns, or refund inquiries:

  • Email: support@pokecards-store.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
  • Order Number Required: Please include in all communications

11. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
